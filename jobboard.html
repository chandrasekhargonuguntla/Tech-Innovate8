An employment website is a website that deals specifically with
employment or careers. Many employment websites are designed to
allow employers to post job requirements for a position to be filled
and are commonly known as job boards. Other employment sites offer employer reviews.

Career and job-search advice, and describe different job descriptions or employers. Through a job website, a prospective employee can locate and fill out a job application or submit resumes over the Internet for the advertised position.
